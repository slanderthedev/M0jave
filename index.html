Mojave

M0jave is an application with the motive to use profiles to allow a user to customize their idevices without a full-fledged jailbreak. All rights below to Code4iOS (@Code4iOS) for the profiles.

Disclaimer

M0jave consists of profiles that are safe and do not harm you device in any way! However if any issues or problems occur we are not responsible for the issue but we will provide support. M0JAVE'S GUI ONLY WORKS CORRECTLY WITH THE IPHONE X RESOLUTION AND SCREEN SIZE FOR NOW! WILL UPDATE WHEN POSSIBLE.

Installation 1. Download the ipa. 2. Download Cydia Impactor. 3. Plug your idevice into your computer. 4. Drag and drop the ipa into Cydia Impactor. 5. Sign in with you Apple ID. 6. Wait. 7. Profit?
